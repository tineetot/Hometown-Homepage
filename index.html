Discover the Heart of Metro Manila: Mandaluyong

Experience the ultimate blend of high-energy urban living and local charm
in the "Tiger City," right at the geographic center of the metropolis.

Top three activities to do at Mandaluyong

Retail Therapy at the Mega-Malls

Mandaluyong is home to some of the largest shopping complexes in the world, specifically SM Megamall and Shangri-La Plaza. Whether you're hunting for high-end luxury brands or exploring the massive "Fashion Hall," you can spend an entire day (and get your 10,000 steps) without leaving the air conditioning.

A Food Crawl Through Highway Hills

Beyond the malls, the district of Highway Hills offers a more intimate vibe. It is packed with hidden coffee shops, local bakeries, and "hole-in-the-wall" diners that offer everything from authentic Filipino comfort food to modern fusion.

Active Recreation at Greenfield District

On weekends, the Greenfield District transforms into an open-air hub. You can visit the Greenfield Weekend Market for artisanal goods and live music, or try indoor rock climbing and "Trampoline Park" for a more athletic afternoon.
